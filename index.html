Spot is your newest training partner. Form is crucial to strength training, so while you work out Spot will make sure you are using the right form with each exercise.

Train Optimally

Maximise your strength and size gains by targeting the correct muscle groups.

Train for Progress

Master the technique and challenge your body with advanced exercises.

Train Safely

Improper form is dangerous and increases the risk of injury by using the wrong muscles.

Train Whenever

Workout on your time and be confident you will be under guidance, all the time.

Spot's Movement Tracking technology highlights the activated muscles for each exercise. If they are outlined in green, you are doing it right!

If you are performing the exercise incorrectly, Spot will outline the breaching body part in yellow and tell you how to correct your form.

If you are at risk of injuring yourself, Spot will instantly notify you and change the tracking outline to red.

Spot keeps count of your reps and sets. When you have finished your set Spot will time your rest and log your progress.

Following each workout, your training results will be synced to Spot's mobile application where you can track your progress and learn how to improve.

Progress your training

As you master your form you unlock Phases which change your training regime to include exercises that challenge your body and help you get stronger.

Get continuing guidance

If you are having trouble with a particular exercise, Spot pinpoints the problem and provides you with tips to help you improve.
